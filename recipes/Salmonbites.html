Salmonbites Recipe

Home

Description

Honey garlic salmon bites roasted over a grill and doused in garlic sauce and hot honey sauce. Cooked medium so that edges are crispy but not overdone in the middle. Served with rice and garnished with sesame seeds and chopped spring onion. 1/2 lb salmon creates about 2 adult servings and includes about 25g protein and 20g carbs.

ingredients

  • Skinned Slamon, (1/2 lb for two servings)
  • Premade rice
  • Garlic Sauce
  • Hot honey Sauce
  • Spring onion for garnish
  • Sesame Seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. Cut Salmon Bites into cubes about an inch long and put into pan with butter
  2. Cook for few minutes on higher heat and add in hot honey and garlic sauce and flip
  3. keep sauteing until crispy on the outside but not black and overdone
  4. Once finished put onto plate with formed rice and sprinkle sesame seeds and shopped spring onions
