Steak Tips Recipe

Home

Description

Steak tips that are premarinated or one can marinate them themself. This recipe will include steps for unmarinated steak tips.Include side of asparagus or green beans and rice or potatos. Cooked on high temp for 5 min to sear the edges and keep juicy flavor inside. 1 lb of steak tips serves about two and contains about 55g protein and 35g carbs

ingredients

  • steak tips
  • Rice cooked with extra water
  • asparagus
  • chives
  • butter & thyme

Steps

  1. Turn heat to high with butter in pan and place steak tips on hot pan
  2. Cook for about 3-4 min and then take lid off and add thyme into pan and baste steak tips on and off for 2 min
  3. Take steak tips off heat and let cool for 2-3 min before serving with rice and asparagus
  4. top off with salt, pepper, and chives for garnish
