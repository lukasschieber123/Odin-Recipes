Steak Tips Recipe

Home

Description

Steak tips marinated in "steakhouse" barbaque sauce and teryaki sauce. Cooked on higher heat to produce rare temperature with a seared perimeter. Served with side of rice or potatoes with a vetetable such as green beans or asparagus. 1 lb of steak tips serves two with about 55g protein and 20g Carbs.

ingredients

  • 1 lb steak premarinated or marinated oneself
  • butter
  • premade rice
  • side of asparagus

Steps

  1. Sear steak on high heat and flip onto each side while basting in butter and marinate juice
  2. if cooked on grill same thing, sear on high temp for 3-5 min until visible lines on steak
  3. let cool for 2-3 min before serving on plate with rice and asparagus
