Farmers Pot Recipe

Home

Description

Farmers pot is a traditional german dish that is generally cooked in a pot with broth however for this recipe it will be done in a pan. It includes a mix of mostly potatos with chopped ham, peppers, carrots, onions, and any other additons of ones choice. Eggs are also added to keep everything together and add flavor. 4 potatos serves about two and contains around 20g protein and 30g carbs

ingredients

  • potatos
  • ham
  • bell peppers (green or red)
  • eggs
  • carrots
  • onions

Steps

  1. prepare about 4 potatos by cutting them into slices and boiling them until cooked
  2. add in potatos, 5-6 eggs, cubed ham or kielbasa, onions, carrots, and peppers.
  3. Cook on medium heat until visibly cooked and staying together.
  4. top off with chives and salt and pepper
